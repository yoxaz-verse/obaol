## Key Solutions Offered

- **Daily Verified Product Listings**: Farmers and FPOs list their products (like turmeric, cardamom, and rice) directly into our platform, visible to verified global buyers.
- **Trade Rooms & Buyer Discovery**: Exporters join curated community rooms (via GAIN) to match with live buyer requirements.
- **Live Market Pricing**: Both parties get access to real-time, trustworthy market prices based on region and commodity.
- **Lead Verification Engine**: Each trade inquiry is screened for intent, volume capacity, and past trade history before sharing.
- **Digital Trade Profiles**: Producers and exporters build reputation via successful trades, certifications, and reviews.
